Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? +

Click on "Sign Up" in the top navigation, fill out the form with your LPU email address, and verify your account through the email we send you.

Is LPU Market free to use? +

Yes! LPU Market is completely free. There are no listing fees, transaction fees, or membership charges.

How do I contact a seller? +

Click on any product to view its details, then click the "Contact Seller" button. You'll need to be logged in to contact sellers.

What if I have issues with a transaction? +

Contact us immediately through this form or email us at support@lpumarket.com. We'll help mediate and resolve any issues.

Can I edit or delete my listings? +

Yes! Go to your profile page where you can edit, delete, or mark your products as sold.
